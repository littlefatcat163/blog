What's the favourite subject of conversation in England?

Where do you come from?

I come from England.

What's the climate like in your country?

It's mild, but it's not always pleasant.

The weather's often cold in the North and windy in the East.
It's often wet in the West and sometimes warm in the South.

Which seasons do you like best?

I like spring and summer.
The days are long and nights are short.
The sun rises early and sets late.

I don't like autumn and winter.
The days are short and the nights are long.
The sun rises late and sets early.
Our climate is not very good, but it's certainly interesting.
It's our favourite subject of conversation.

mild

  1. （天气等）温暖的；暖和的；adj.
    They had an exceptionally mild winter last year. 他们那儿去年冬天出奇地暖和。
    The climate in the South is always mild and pleasant. 南方的气候总是温和宜人。

  2. （性情等）温和的；adj.
    John is a mild man who never raises his voice. 约翰是一个温和的人，他从不太高嗓门说话。
    I like his gentle and mild voice. 我喜欢他那温和而轻柔的声音。

  3. （食物等）淡味的；adj.
    Try this mild curry. 试试这种淡味咖喱。
    He likes to smoke mild cigars. 他喜欢抽淡味雪茄烟。

rise

  1. （日、月）升起；v.
    The sun rises in the east. 太阳从东方升起。
    The curtain rises at 8:00 p.m.

  2. （河水、物价、温度等）上涨；升高；增加；v.
    The level of the river is still rising. 河水水位仍在上涨。

  3. 起床；起身；v.
    My mother always rises early. 我妈妈总是很早起床。
    The house rose to the singers. 全场起立向歌唱演员们致意。
